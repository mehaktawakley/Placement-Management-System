Tips For Students

What could be better than getting placed soon after graduation!? However, there are few requirements to sit for placements and some tips to clear the selection process.

The Sooner the Better

There are always some criteria such as minimum graduation percentage, number of backlogs etc to sit for placements. Students should work accordingly since 1st year.

Know Your Values

You should know what you are and how valuable you can be for an orgaization. Your skills will always help you to grow.

Learn About Yourself

No one knows you better than you. You should know your strengths and weaknesses as they can be helpful. Work on your weaknesses.

Identify Your Skills

No two persons have same set of skills. A person strong in managing may not be good in coding & vice versa. Thus it becomes important to identify your own set of skills.

Research Career Options

The world is vast and thus are your career options. You should do some good research and identify the interest areas.

Link Your Major & Your Career

Your gratuation is the phase where you devote time and prepare for your future.
